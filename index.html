About GeNext About GeNext

Built With You, For You.

We’re creating this framework with real businesses at the heart of it. Every company that registers to get certified contributes to the evolution of our blueprint for better business, helping shape a standard that’s practical, relevant, and grounded in real-world values.

  • A modern certification for small and local businesses committed to purpose, integrity, and impact.
  • Built on three pillars—Customers, Culture, and Community—that recognise real values in action.
  • Join a trusted network of like-minded organisations driving positive change in business and beyond.
Read More

GeNext is a modern certification for small and local businesses that want to do better — for their customers, their culture, and their community. We recognise real values in action, not just policies on paper.

Most certifications are expensive, complex, and built for big businesses. GeNext is designed to be affordable, accessible, and relevant to SMEs and local enterprises—while still being rigorous, meaningful, and rooted in impact.

GeNext is for independent businesses, startups, social enterprises, and community-led companies that care about how they work, not just what they sell. If you believe in running your business with integrity and heart, you’re in the right place.

Certification starts with a short set of qualifying questions covering our three pillars—Customers, Culture, and Community. From there, we guide you through a light-touch process to assess, support, and celebrate your impact as a values-led business.

We know that impact doesn’t always show up on spreadsheets. While we may ask for supporting details where needed, our process is designed to be conversational, collaborative, and focused on understanding your business—not testing it.

You’ll receive a GeNext Certified badge for your website, emails, and socials; a profile in our Impact Network directory; and early access to resources, events, and insights to help you keep growing as a purpose-led business.
